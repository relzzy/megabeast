A Technological Marvel for Your Next Event

Robot Rock is a fully interactive walking robot that brings high-tech energy to any room. From the moment he arrives, he commands attention with loud servos and heavy footsteps as he struts in — turning every head and sparking excitement across the venue. This isn't just a costume; it's a complete sensory experience designed to immerse your guests in the future.

Your Robot Rock Booking — What to Expect

Arrival and Setup

Our costume and equipment arrive in two large trunks (approx 1m × 50cm × 50cm). The performer will need a private dressing room to apply stage makeup, dress, and get ready. We require a secure space to store the boxes, which double as a workstation for minor repairs between sets.

Robot Rock Performances

The standard booking includes 3 × 30 minute sets inside the robot suit. To ensure safety and high energy, we require at least a 45-minute rest period between sets. Because the suit is around 2 meters tall, we need a performance space with at least 2.5 meters of height clearance. Due to reduced vision and the bulky nature of the costume, the operator/minder acts as an escort to ensure the robot doesn't bump into hazards or get overcrowded by guests.

What We Require on the Day

  • Free parking nearby for a Medium Wheelbase panel van (5m length, 2.5m height).
  • A clear, safe walkway between the dressing area and the performance space.
  • Light refreshments and water throughout the day.
  • If we are on-site for 5 or more hours, a hot meal must be provided.
